Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

450 Calories
18g Protein
55g Carbs
18g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup chickpeas, drained
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley and mint
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa in vegetable broth according to package instructions. Let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, chickpeas, olives, and feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour over the quinoa mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley and mint before serving.
