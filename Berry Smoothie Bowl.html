Berry Smoothie Bowl

320 Calories
15g Protein
58g Carbs
6g Fat

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Toppings: fresh berries, granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes, sliced almonds

Instructions

  1. Add frozen banana, frozen berries, Greek yogurt, almond milk, and honey to a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth and thick. Add more milk if needed for desired consistency.
  3. Pour into a bowl.
  4. Arrange toppings in sections: fresh berries, granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes, and sliced almonds.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy with a spoon.
