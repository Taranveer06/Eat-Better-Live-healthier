Hearty Lentil Stew

280 Calories
18g Protein
45g Carbs
5g Fat

Ingredients

  • 2 cups green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 2 cups spinach or kale
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© onion, carrots, and celery until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic, cumin, and smoked paprika. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and bay leaves.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-35 minutes until lentils are tender.
  6. Stir in spinach or kale and cook until wilted.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove bay leaves, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve with crusty bread.
